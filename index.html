Our Heritage

Serving Our Community For Over 60 Years

Since 1960, Gulf Shores Service Center has been the backbone of automotive care on the Alabama Gulf Coast. What started as a small roadside garage has grown into the most trusted name in towing and auto repair.

Three generations of our family have dedicated themselves to keeping this community on the road. From the earliest days of helping stranded motorists on unpaved coastal roads to today's state-of-the-art diagnostic equipment, our commitment remains unchanged: honest service, fair prices, and treating every customer like family.

Located at the heart of Gulf Shores on East 2nd Street, we've watched our town grow from a quiet fishing village to a vibrant coastal destination. Through hurricanes, economic changes, and technological revolutions, one thing has stayed constant—we're here when you need us, 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.
